Useful Statistics

Get statistics about your resources that you wouldn't normally be able to access. Statistics are offered from a high level to give a quick glance regarding the most popular resource or there are more detailed statistics at hand that can be used to make business decisions. Track your most used resources or your app as a whole by viewing details about where requests are coming from and when.

Use our GeoIP tool that will allow you to view where in the world your data is being requested from. If linked with a mobile app, this can give insights into the geographic popularity of your application.





User Groups

Control what resources can be accessed by what user groups. Create a user group, bind API Keys to that group and then choose what groups can access a particular resource.

Create user groups for all levels or just for particular resources.
